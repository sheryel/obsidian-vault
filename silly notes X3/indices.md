# indices
the index is $y$ in $x^y$ 
it means that $x$ is multiplied by itself $y$ times

> [!NOTE] 
> $x^1 = x$ 
## Operations on indices
[[silly notes X3/operations on indices]]
$$a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}$$
$$a^m \div a^n = a^{m-n}$$
$$a^0 = 1$$
$$a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n}$$
$$(a^m)^n = a^{m \cdot n}$$
$$
a^{-n} = \frac{1}{a^n}
$$

> [!tip] 
> $m$ and $n$ can be any value
